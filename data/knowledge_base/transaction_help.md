# Transaction Help

## Failed Transfers

Failed transfers are usually reversed within 24–48 hours.

## Pending Transfers

Transfers may remain pending due to network or interbank processing delays.

## Double Debits

Customers who experience double debits should raise a dispute for investigation.

## Wrong Beneficiary Transfers

Recovery requests can be initiated, but successful recovery depends on the receiving institution and beneficiary cooperation.

## Transaction Disputes

Customers should provide their transaction reference when reporting transaction issues.
