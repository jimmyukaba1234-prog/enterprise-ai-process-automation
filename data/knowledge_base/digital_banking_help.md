# Digital Banking Help

## Password Reset

Customers can reset passwords through the mobile app or internet banking portal.

## Login Issues

Customers experiencing login issues should verify their credentials and internet connectivity.

## Mobile Banking

Customers can:

* Transfer Funds
* Pay Bills
* Check Account Balance
* View Transaction History

## Internet Banking

Provides access to account management, transfers, and service requests.

Customers should never share passwords, PINs, or OTPs with anyone.
