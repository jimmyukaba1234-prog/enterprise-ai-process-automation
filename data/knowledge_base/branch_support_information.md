# Branch and Customer Support Information Guide

## Purpose

This document provides information regarding branch services, customer support channels, operating hours, and general service inquiries.

The AI Customer Operations Agent may use this information to answer general questions that do not require customer account access.

---

## General Information Requests

Examples include:

- Branch locations
- Branch operating hours
- Contact center information
- ATM locations
- Service availability
- Product information
- Complaint submission channels

Verification is generally not required for these inquiries.

---

## Branch Services

Branches may provide:

- Account opening
- KYC updates
- Card collection
- Account maintenance
- Complaint resolution
- Loan inquiries
- Business banking support

Some services may require prior appointment or verification.

---

## Branch Operating Hours

Standard operating hours may vary by location.

Customers should be advised to:

- Check official channels
- Contact customer support
- Confirm branch-specific schedules

before visiting.

---

## ATM Services

Customers may request information regarding:

- ATM locations
- ATM cash withdrawal services
- Cardless withdrawal availability
- ATM dispute processes

The AI agent should provide available guidance and escalation paths where necessary.

---

## Customer Support Channels

Customers may contact support through:

- Mobile banking application
- Internet banking portal
- Contact center
- Official email support
- Branch visits
- Official WhatsApp banking service

---

## Complaint Submission Channels

Customers may submit complaints through:

- Contact center
- Mobile app support
- Internet banking support
- Branch service desk
- Official complaint email channels

---

## Service Availability Information

The AI agent may provide guidance regarding:

- Transfer services
- Card services
- KYC services
- Account maintenance services
- Loan application channels

---

## Escalation Rules

Escalate when:

- Branch-specific information is unavailable
- Customer requests specialist support
- Service outage is suspected
- Customer complaint requires investigation

Department:

Customer Operations

---

## Allowed Actions

The AI agent may:

- Provide branch information
- Provide support channel information
- Explain service availability
- Route customers appropriately

The AI agent may NOT:

- Invent branch information
- Provide unavailable branch schedules
- Confirm unsupported services

---

## Customer Communication Example

> You can access this service through our branch network, digital banking channels, or customer support team. If you require location-specific information, I can help direct your request to the appropriate support channel.

---

## AI Agent Instruction Summary

1. Identify request type.
2. Determine whether verification is needed.
3. Provide available information.
4. Route customer where necessary.
5. Escalate when information is unavailable.
6. Maintain service accuracy.
